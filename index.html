Real time detection of water flow and contaminants transport in the subsurface

Sensoil's Vadose zone Monitoring System (VMSTM) are specially designed for a wide range of environmental and industrial applications including:

  • Protection of groundwater resources from pollution hazard
  • Optimization of subsurface remediation
  • Management of heap-leach mining
  • Early warning on earthen dams stability
  • Control of agricultural inputs down-leaching of
  • Land-fill waste management

Sensoil advanced vadose-zone monitoring system is currently the only technology available for real time detection of water flow and contaminants transport in deep unsaturated sediment.
